About Us

Founded with a vision to serve and protect cows

Founded with a vision to serve and protect cows, Premadhu Gau Seva Samiti is a non-profit organization committed to Gau Seva (cow service). Our gaushala provides a safe haven for cows, offering them fresh grass, clean water, nutritious feed, and medical attention.

We also work to support local communities through education, organic farming initiatives, and sustainable dairy practices. Our commitment extends beyond cow welfare to environmental sustainability and community development.

Our Mission

Protecting and serving Gau Mata with compassion

At Premadhu Gau Seva Samiti, we are dedicated to the protection, care, and welfare of cows, revered as Gau Mata in our culture. Inspired by the teachings of compassion and service, our mission is to provide shelter, nourishment, and medical care to stray, abandoned, and injured cows, ensuring they live with dignity and respect.

We aim to promote sustainable practices and raise awareness about the ecological and spiritual significance of cows in our society and environment.

Why Gau Seva?

In Indian tradition, cows are considered the embodiment of Kamadhenu, the divine cow of plenty. They symbolize gentleness, nurturing, and selfless service. By serving Gau Mata, we honor our cultural heritage and contribute to environmental sustainability through organic farming and natural resource utilization.

Our Work

Comprehensive care and community service

Rescue and Rehabilitation

We rescue abandoned, injured, or sick cows and provide them with proper care and rehabilitation in our gaushala.

Medical Care

Our team ensures timely medical treatment for injured and ailing cows with veterinary support.

Organic Farming

We promote eco-friendly farming by utilizing cow dung and urine for organic fertilizers and natural products.

Community Outreach

We organize awareness programs, health camps, and educational initiatives to highlight the importance of cow protection.
